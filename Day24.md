# Day 24 – AI Career Platform Investment Dashboard

## 📚 Topic Covered
- Analyzed an AI Career Platform using an investment diligence dashboard.
- Evaluated business model, revenue strategy, go-to-market plan, competitive moat, risks, and investment readiness.

## 🎯 Key Learnings
- A strong problem alone is not enough; customer validation is essential.
- Revenue assumptions must be supported by paying customers.
- Distribution and outcome-based data create long-term competitive advantages.
- Early-stage startups should focus on proving product-market fit before scaling.

## 🛠️ Worked On
- Reviewed the business model and projected revenue streams.
- Studied the go-to-market execution plan.
- Assessed key risks and investment scorecard.
- Learned how investors evaluate startup viability.

## 💡 Highlights
- Business Viability: **58/100**
- Revenue Potential: **42/100**
- GTM Strength: **38/100**
- Competitive Strength: **35/100**
- Investor Readiness: **31/100**
- Final Verdict: **Validate — Not Yet Investable**

## ✅ Outcome
Gained practical insights into startup evaluation, investment analysis, customer validation, and business strategy using an AI startup case study.
